IKATS in 60 words...

IKATS (Innovative Tool-Kit for Analysing Time Series) is the result of a [R&D Data Science Project](/genesis.html) and has been carried out in collaboration with the LIG (Laboratoire Informatique de Grenoble) team.

IKATS provides a READY2USE toolkit for the handling, exploratory analysis and visualization of large volumes of Time Series (TS) data within a single Big Data framework.

For a general overview, please, take a look at the IKATS booklet.

If you are interested in a second time to investigate and interact with IKATS without get involved in a long installation process, let start play with the sandbox :

Beautiful, Fast, Efficient, simple and powerful Timeseries Data Analytics

Data Scientists, Data Engineers use IKATS to explore and generate predictive models making it possible on a second hand to supervise manufacturing industry production lines or to help operate hi-tech industrial systems relying on networks of sensors.

Whatever industries you work on like aeronautics, energy, rail and manufacturing, analysing, exploring and elaborating predictive models from Time Series is a burden in the Data Science domain. With the introduction of IKATS, you can now "keep your eyes" on your data.
